abSENT - an SMS service for NPS students.

FAQ:

What is it?:

abSENT is a notification service written in Python, that sends you a text message whenever your teachers are abSENT. abSENT is a hobby project of Roshan Karim (NNHS '24) and Kevin Yang (NSHS '24), who got tired of refreshing a website constantly in the morning. Because of this inter-school colloboration, abSENT works for both North and South.

How does it work?:

User's text "SUBSCRIBE" to our phone number, after which an interactive subscription process is commenced. Don't let the word fool you: abSENT is completely free. Once we recieve all the information we need, we listen every morning for the absence list and let you know if your teachers are on it. For you, it's that simple!

Okay, but really, why?:

After going to Newton High Schools and experiencing the absence list for two years, something had to be done. It's an annoying way of learning about absent teachers, it's often posted very late, and it's never posted at a consistent time. In the morning, when other things need to get done, this isn't acceptable. Thus, abSENT.

How can I help?:

Whilst the codebase is mostly in a maintenance state right now and help isn't need with it, there are others ways to help. Reach out to us if you notice a bug or regression. And of course, if you can, donate to help us out with the server costs.

More info:

  • abSENT will only message you when you have abSENT teachers. On days where all of your teachers are present, no message is sent.
  • Occasionally, abSENT will push announcements including information on new feature updates or general goings-on within the schools.
  • Unfortunately, the basis of abSENT is teacher names. When signing up, be sure to type your teacher's names correctly! If this isn't done, you won't get notifications.
